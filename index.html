How we started

In 1995 a group of professional and experienced engineers decided to join forces to create a construction company of its kind. With trust, courage, integrity and grace of Allah Almighty company grows and shapes into a leading project and construction management company. Such phenomenal expansion in such a short time period has been achieved through hard work, devotion to the cause and sincerity to the organization by our work force and management.

We are now shaping ourselves to become a one stop solution for a number of industrial sectors and to support our goals we have employed the best and support a portfolio of brands that are leaders in the industry with vast experience in their respective domains.
